# Market Research for GenBridge

## Overview
GenBridge is a reverse mentorship platform that connects Gen Z experts with professionals seeking to enhance their digital skills. This document outlines the market research conducted to support the business model and strategic direction of GenBridge.

## Market Size
The global online education market is projected to reach $375 billion by 2026, growing at a CAGR of 8.23%. The demand for mentorship and skill development is increasing, particularly in the tech sector, where rapid advancements necessitate continuous learning.

## Target Audience
- **Gen Z Individuals**: Aged 18-24, tech-savvy, and eager to share their knowledge.
- **Professionals**: Aged 30-55, looking to upskill and adapt to new technologies.
- **Corporates**: Organizations seeking to enhance employee skills and foster a culture of continuous learning.

## Competitor Analysis
1. **MentorCruise**
   - Focus: Tech mentorship
   - Pricing: $50/session
   - Unique Selling Proposition: Personalized mentorship matching based on skills and goals.

2. **GrowthMentor**
   - Focus: Business and marketing mentorship
   - Pricing: $100/month for unlimited sessions
   - Unique Selling Proposition: Access to a network of experienced mentors across various industries.

3. **LinkedIn Learning**
   - Focus: Online courses and professional development
   - Pricing: $29.99/month
   - Unique Selling Proposition: Extensive library of courses with industry-recognized certifications.

## Insights
- **Demand for Digital Skills**: There is a significant gap in digital skills among professionals, particularly in areas like AI, social media, and blockchain.
- **Preference for Peer Learning**: Many professionals prefer learning from peers who are more familiar with current trends and technologies.
- **Flexibility in Learning**: The ability to choose mentors based on specific skills and availability is crucial for users.

## Conclusion
The mentorship market is ripe for disruption, and GenBridge is well-positioned to capitalize on the growing demand for digital skills training. By leveraging the unique strengths of Gen Z mentors and offering flexible, personalized learning experiences, GenBridge can effectively meet the needs of its target audience.
